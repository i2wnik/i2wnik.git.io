What is DressTuning?

DressTuning is a leading company for customization of clothes.
What is the customization of clothing?
Customization of clothing is the tuning of things, individualization for a particular person with the help of improvised materials,
for example, paints or threads. The object of customization can be any thing: an old denim jacket, and a newly purchased T-shirt.
Improvised materials can be spikes, jewelry, paint, stripes and much more.
Customization helps to create your own unique thing and unique style in clothing.
DressTuning was founded in 2020 and during this period has earned many awards and positive reviews.
